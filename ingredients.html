Foundation of every butter

The shared base

Ingredient
What it brings to your skin
Unrefined Shea Butter
The heart of the formula. Rich in fatty acids that deeply nourish and soften skin.
Ethically sourced from women's cooperatives in West Africa, where shea has been a cornerstone of skin care for generations. Supports the skin barrier, soothes irritation, and keeps moisture in without clogging pores.
Unrefined Cocoa Butter
Gives the butter its structure and a long, slow release of moisture.
Traditionally valued for its ability to support skin elasticity and soften the appearance of stretch marks over time. Creates a protective layer that holds hydration close to the skin.
Fractionated Coconut Oil
The lightweight carrier that makes the butter absorb cleanly without greasiness.
Stays liquid at room temperature. Penetrates quickly, helps other ingredients absorb, and leaves a soft, non-sticky finish on the skin.
Jojoba Oil
Technically a wax ester - the closest plant oil to the skin's own natural sebum.
Balances oil production, supports skin that tends toward dryness or sensitivity, and absorbs without residue. Stable, non-comedogenic, and long-treasured in plant medicine traditions.
Vitamin E
A plant-derived antioxidant that protects the formula and supports the look of healthy skin.
Helps shield the skin's surface from environmental stress, supports an even-looking complexion, and extends the shelf life of the butter naturally - without synthetic preservatives.

This base is the same in every Atabey Botanica body butter. What makes each scent distinct is the botanical added at the finish.

Wild Shield
Bug Barrier  |  Botanical Repellent  ·  Summer Seasonal
✦
A botanical blend of citronella, cedarwood, lemongrass, and rosemary - each chosen for their traditional use in repelling mosquitoes and ticks. Whipped light into the butter so it absorbs cleanly, leaves no greasy residue, and wears comfortably in summer heat. Protection you will actually want to put on.

Wild Shield shares the same nourishing base as every Atabey Botanica butter. It adds two more plant ingredients - Sesame Seed Oil and Arrowroot Powder - and a purposeful blend of essential oils, each chosen for what it repels and what it brings to the skin.

Additional base ingredients in Wild Shield
Sesame Seed Oil
Rich in antioxidants and naturally protective. Absorbs well and adds a soft, skin-conditioning layer.
Arrowroot Powder
A fine plant starch that absorbs excess moisture on the skin. Gives the butter a dry, silky finish - no greasiness, even in summer heat.
